# Privacy Policy

Video 3x Speed does not collect, transmit, sell, or share user data.

The extension runs locally in the browser and changes the playback speed of HTML5 video elements on pages the user visits. It stores only three preferences through Chrome's storage API:

- whether the extension is enabled
- the selected playback speed
- the selected hold-to-speed shortcut speed

No browsing history, page content, video content, keyboard input, account information, analytics, or personally identifiable information is collected by this extension.
